# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 5, 2024
**Last Updated:** November 5, 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to DreamNoteApp ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Your dreams are deeply personal, and we understand the sensitive nature of the content you share with our app. This policy outlines our commitment to keeping your dream journal private and secure.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

- **Profile Data:** App preferences, settings, and subscription status
- **Payment Information:** Processed securely through App Store/Google Play (we do not store payment details)

### Dream Content

- **Dream Entries:** All text, emotions, tags, and metadata you add to your dreams
- **Usage Patterns:** Frequency of app usage, feature usage statistics
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version for technical support

### Automatically Collected Data

- **Technical Data:** Crash reports, performance metrics, and error logs
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage statistics to improve app functionality

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality

- **Dream Storage:** Securely store and organize your dream entries
- **AI Analysis:** Process dream content for interpretation
- **Synchronization:** Sync your data across multiple devices
- **Personalization:** Customize your app experience based on preferences

### Service Improvement

- **App Enhancement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- **Technical Support:** Diagnose and resolve technical issues
- **Security:** Monitor for suspicious activity and prevent unauthorized access

### Communication

- **Support Responses:** Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- **Service Updates:** Notify you of important app updates or changes
- **Premium Features:** Information about subscription benefits and renewals

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- **Primary Storage:** All dream data is encrypted and stored locally on your device using AES-256 encryption
- **Offline Access:** Your dreams remain accessible even without internet connection
- **Device Security:** Data is protected by your device's built-in security features

### Cloud Backup

- **Optional Service:** Cloud backup is only available
- **End-to-End Encryption:** All data is encrypted before leaving your device
- **Secure Transmission:** Data transfer uses TLS 1.3 encryption
- **Limited Access:** Only you can decrypt and access your cloud-stored dreams

### Security Measures

- **Encryption:** Bank-level AES-256 encryption for all dream content
- **Access Controls:** Multi-factor authentication and secure access protocols
- **Regular Audits:** Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Data Isolation:** Each user's data is completely isolated from others

---

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Share Your Dreams

- **Zero Sharing:** Your dream content is never shared with third parties
- **No Marketing Use:** We do not use your dreams for advertising or marketing
- **No AI Training:** Your dreams are not used to train external AI models
- **No Data Sales:** We never sell your personal information or dream data

### Limited Exceptions

We may disclose information only in these specific circumstances:

- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or government request
- **Safety Concerns:** To prevent harm to you or others
- **Service Providers:** To trusted partners who help operate our service (under strict confidentiality)
- **Business Transfer:** In case of merger or acquisition (with continued privacy protection)

### AI Processing

- **Local First:** Basic analysis happens on your device when possible
- **Secure Processing:** AI analysis uses encrypted, anonymous data processing
- **No Retention:** AI services do not store or learn from your specific dream content
- **Opt-In Only:** AI features require explicit consent and Premium subscription
